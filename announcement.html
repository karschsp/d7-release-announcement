After two and a half years of development by over 1,000 contributors, we proudly present Drupal 7: the best version of Drupal yet.

Download

Easy to use

An entirely revamped administrative interface makes getting started with Drupal incredibly easy. Customize workflows with the dashboard, toolbar and shortcuts features. Update modules and themes directly from within Drupal.

Flexible content

Define your own content types with the new Field API. Add custom data fields to a variety of content including users, comments, taxonomies and more.

Images and files

Drupal 7 supports image handling out of the box. Resize and alter your images with ease. Combine private/public files and store files directly using services such as Amazon S3.

Built to scale

Optimize your site to serve your visitors the information they need at the fastest speeds possible with support for advanced caching, master/slave replication, reverse-proxy and content delivery networks.

Upgrading

Your Drupal 6 site can be upgraded to Drupal 7, once Drupal 7 versions of all modules and themes it uses are available. Over 800 modules are already there for Drupal 7.

Test driven development

A code testing framework with 28,000 tests, a testing module and continuous integration testing of all Drupal core patches and selected contributed modules. This is the most secure and stable version of Drupal ever.

Database support

A new database layer lets you go beyond MySQL and postgreSQL: SQLite is supported out of the box and there is support for MS SQL Server, Oracle, MongoDB and more.

New Render API

Requirements

  • Web Server: Apache (recommended), Nginx, Lighttpd or Microsoft IIS
  • Database: MySQL 5.0.15 and higher, PostgreSQL, 8.3 and higher, SQLite 3.x
  • PHP: 5.2.x or 5.3
  • Memory: 32MB
